About Donut Girls



Tradition since 2019

We are a homebased licensed business that produces the authentic Pasteis de Nata, most known as Portuguese tarts. Thierry's background is Portuguese, Lived and worked as a baker in Portugal for serveral years, so we decided to make and open the only Real Pasteis de Nata in Canberra. Both trained by Portuguese bakers we bring you sweets that your tooth craves. We also produce other sweets and deserts. Why we are called Donut Girls? Our dog is called Donut and Jamie decided to open a bakery, so we named it Donut girls. So If you love sweets, we've got you covered.
